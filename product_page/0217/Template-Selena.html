Selena Modern Style

The Selena modular tufted sectional is a modern sectional that embodies smart furniture. It offers customizable features such as cup holders, LED lights, USB ports, and a bed function. With its modern style sofa, the sectional can complement any contemporary living space. Its tufted detailing adds an extra layer of sophistication to its design, while its modular feature allows for various layouts to fit any room. Available in fabric and leather, most covers are detachable and washable, making it easy to clean and maintain. Overall, the Selena modular tufted sectional is a stylish and practical addition to any modern living space.
