Tungfu Modern Fabric Sectional

Tungfu modern sectional uses shiny nylon hybrid fabric, giving it a very relaxing feel and luxurious look. The sectional is designed to be reconfigured, rearranged, and moved around. With the five individual pieces of Tungfu, you can create different room layouts and more.


This modern sectional is an eclectic piece that provides an eye-catching and inviting appearance. The multi-colored sectional with a low to the ground presentation showcases an awe-inspiring European design, and the low to the floor design uses shades of navy and grey for a vibrant look. The varied colors of the pillows blended well with the mint and yellow hues of the sofa, giving it an exciting, outstanding appeal. Pillows are included to match the colors and fabric of the sofa. 


Along with its fun color scheme, it features beautifully tufted upholstery and will provide inspiration to your living space. Comfortable seating for six people, this is definitely a piece of furniture that will liven up any room. Sink into the softness that is the Tungfu modern sectional. The contorting fabric wraps around your body to provide the maximum support to feel as though you are on a cloud. 

 

Sets includes 1 Sectional - 2 Ottomans + 2 Armless Chair, 1 Corner Seat + 3 Large Pillows ( Decorative items are not included).
